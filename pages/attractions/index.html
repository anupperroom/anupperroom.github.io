Attractions and Activities

An Upper Room is close to :

  • The University of Natal (Howard College Campus),
  • Entabeni and St. Augustine Hospitals
  • Glenwood High School and Durban Girls High are within close proximity.
  • The Absa Stadium, as well as the Moses Mabhida Stadium are about 10 km from our establishment.
  • There are a number of restaurants and take-aways around the area which offer a variety of different cuisines. Many of the take-aways have in place a delivery service to your door.
  • We are well situated and in close proximity of Musgrave Centre as well as the trendy Pavilion in Westville. Gateway Shopping Centre is approximately 20 km from our B&B and which offers loads of entertainment for the whole family.
  • There are safe water activities for adults and children alike such as surfing and boogie boarding in wave pools and much more especially for children.
